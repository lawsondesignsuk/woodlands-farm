The Team

Meet The Girls

A big welcome from the Woodlands Farm Chickens

Here at Woodlands Farm we would be nothing without our girls, they work tirelessly to produce us the eggs we need to supply all of you guys! They have acres upon acres of land to graze and explore, enjoying everyday and playing all the time!

Meet The Team

A big welcome from the Woodlands Farm Team

Our team at Woodlands Farm are a hard working bunch who take pride in packing and delivering your eggs for you 5 days a week. We have girls who have worked for us for over 18 years, lads who are just starting their tenure and the boss who built the business from the ground up from the age of 18.
